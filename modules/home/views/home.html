TOWARDS SDG 16:

Promoting Just, Peaceful and Inclusive Societies in Europe and Central Asia

In the Europe and Central Asia region, UNDP works with local, national and international partners to strengthen inclusive and effective governance and peace, by advocating, advising, enabling discussion, fostering consensus and capacitating institutions. This website, which will be updated on an ongoing basis, provides insights into UNDP’s governance and peacebuilding work, and will help to assess and measure progress as countries across the region work towards the Sustainable Development Goals - with a focus on goals 5 and 16.
