Promoting Just, Peaceful and Inclusive Societies in Europe and Central Asia

In the Europe and Central Asia region, UNDP works with partners to strengthen inclusive and effective governance and sustain peace. This platform provides insights into how our work helps to make progress from 2015 onwards towards the realization of Sustainable Development Goals – with a focus on SDGs 5 & 16 – by 2030.
